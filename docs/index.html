Quiet your AI chat sidebars.

Hush blurs the conversation titles in ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini so you can leave a chat open in a meeting, on a shared screen, or in public.

Install in four steps

~30 seconds
01

Download the file above

It's a small zip. Most browsers unzip automatically. If yours doesn't, double-click Hush.zip. You'll get a folder called Hush.

02

Open Chrome's extensions page

Browsers won't let websites jump there for you. Paste it in yourself.

chrome://extensions
03

Turn on Developer mode

A toggle lives in the top-right corner of the extensions page. Flip it on.

04

Click Load unpacked and pick the Hush folder

Done. Visit ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Sidebar titles are blurred. Hover any row to reveal it.

Nothing leaves your browser.

Hush runs entirely on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no servers, no account. The whole extension is 42 KB and about 600 lines of code you can read on GitHub in a single sitting.
