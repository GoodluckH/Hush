Quiet your AI chat sidebars.

Hush blurs the conversation titles in ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini so you can leave a chat open in a meeting, on a shared screen, or in public — without leaking context.

Install in 4 steps

1
Download the file above
It's a small zip. Double-click to unzip it — you'll get a folder called chrome-mv3.
2
Open Chrome's extensions page
Paste this in your address bar: chrome://extensions
3
Turn on Developer mode
There's a toggle in the top-right corner of the extensions page. Flip it on.
4
Click "Load unpacked" → pick the chrome-mv3 folder
Done. Visit ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — your sidebar titles are blurred. Hover any row to reveal it. Use ⌘⇧Y to toggle (or Ctrl⇧Y on Windows).

Hush runs entirely on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no servers. The whole extension is 42 KB and ~600 lines of code you can read on GitHub.
